Today, it was a national holiday that gave me a shout of Golden Week. I took an English class in the morning and spaced out for a while. Since we didn't have breakfast, we hit the streets and grabbed curry lunch boxes at our go-to restaurant. Then, I didn't wanna be a couch potato, we headed to the sports shop in Okachimachi. I got there on foot but my partner was by bicycle.

We checked zacks to put on while running from Salomon, and asked the staff for advice about the size. I weigh in on the size for my partner's zack but at the end of the day, I left the decision up to her. However, whichever she will take, it makes the cut, according to the reviews.

On the way to home, I went the extra mile. I was on top speed enought to leave my partner's bicycle. 

I've been able to keep my routine, not ending up being all talk.

### 4. 文法・自然さフィードバック 📝

| # | 原文 | 修正案 | ポイント |
|---|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | "a national holiday that gave me a shout of Golden Week" | "a national holiday that **kicked off** Golden Week" | "give sb a shout" は人に連絡する意味 |
| 2 | "**Then**, I didn't wanna be a couch potato, we headed to..." | "**Not wanting to be** a couch potato, we headed to..." / "**Since** I didn't wanna be a couch potato, we headed..." | 接続が不自然。分詞構文か接続詞で繋ぐ |
| 3 | "my partner was **by bicycle**" | "my partner **rode her bicycle**" / "my partner **came by bicycle**" | "was by bicycle" は不自然 |
| 4 | "I **weigh** in on" | "I **weighed** in on" | 過去形にする |
| 5 | "whichever she **will take**" | "whichever she **takes**" | 条件節では現在形を使う |
| 6 | "On the way **to** home" | "On the way **home**" | "home" は副詞なので前置詞 "to" は不要 |
| 7 | "I was **on** top speed **enought** to leave my partner's bicycle" | "I ran **at** top speed, fast enough to **outpace** my partner **on her** bicycle" | 前置詞は "at"、"enough" のスペルミス、"leave" → "outpace" が自然 |

### 5. 良かった点 🌟

- **leave sth up to sb** の使い方が完璧！ "I left the decision up to her" は文法もニュアンスもネイティブレベル
- **all talk** を日記の締めとして使うセンスが良い。「口だけじゃなく行動してる自分」を表現できている
- **couch potato** も自然な文脈で使えている。「〜になりたくないから動いた」という流れが上手い
- 1つの日記に8つもフレーズを盛り込んだ積極性が素晴らしい！

### 6. 次回のチャレンジ 🎯

**run sth by sb**（～に確認してもらう）をぜひ次回使ってみて：
> "I ran the size options by the staff before deciding."
> （決める前にサイズの選択肢をスタッフに確認してもらった）

**downplay sth**（～を控えめに言う）も日常で使いやすい：
> "I tried to downplay how tired I was, but my legs were killing me."
> （疲れてるのを隠そうとしたけど、脚がパンパンだった）
