### 4. 文法・自然さフィードバック 📝

| # | 原文 | 修正案 | ポイント |
|---|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | "I **let me** off the hook" | "I **let myself** off the hook" | 主語と目的語が同じ人 → 再帰代名詞 myself |
| 2 | "but **drag** is **drag**" | "but **a drag** is **a drag**" | 可算名詞なので冠詞 a が必要 |
| 3 | "I killed **a small tasks**" | "I knocked out **a few small tasks**" | a + 複数形は不一致。"killed" より "knocked out" / "took care of" が自然 |

| 5 | "that made me sure **coming back** again" | "that made me sure **I'd come back**" | made me sure + (that) 節。または "made me want to come back" |
| 6 | "Real talk, **W**hen I joined" | "Real talk, **w**hen I joined" | ピリオドの後ではないので小文字 |
| 7 | "he was in my **parents** house" | "he was **at** my **parents'** house" | 所有格の 's + 前置詞は at が自然 |
| 8 | "making me **somewhat awkward**" | "**which made it** somewhat awkward" / "making **me feel** awkward" | "making me awkward" だと「私を変な人にした」になる |
