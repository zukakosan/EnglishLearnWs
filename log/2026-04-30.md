### 主な文法フィードバック
- "I let me off the hook" → "I let myself off the hook"（再帰代名詞）
- "drag is drag" → "a drag is a drag"（冠詞）
- "I killed a small tasks" → "I knocked out a few small tasks"（a + 複数形の不一致）
- "put my legs up" → "put my feet up"（イディオムの間違い）
- "making me somewhat awkward" → "which made it somewhat awkward"（構文）
- "It was the first time to use" → "It was my first time using"（動名詞）
- "my start point" → "my starting point"（複合語）
- **draw the line (#269)**: set the boundary は近いが慣用句は **draw the line**
- **gross sb out (#12)**: gloss ≠ gross。gross = 気持ち悪い、gloss = 光沢
- **bring back memories (#84)**: throwback は名詞「懐かしいもの」、bring back memories は動詞句「思い出がよみがえる」
- **mull over sth (#363)**: contemplate はフォーマル、カジュアルでは **mull over** が自然
