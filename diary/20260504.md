In the morning I overslept a bit since I stayed up late last night for playing APEX Legends. To put my mind to productive things, I decided to run to anywhere. Some places popped into my mind to visit, but I had second thoughts and decided to go to Ochano-mizu area, which is 4.2km far from my residence.

In Ochano-mizu area, I bumped into a small festival which seemed a parade of locals. They carried Mikoshi, singing and moving their bodies. I had no clue that there would be festival but I felt lucky to see that as a visitor.

First, I thought I can eat Okinawan soba, but no luck. The restaurant was closed due to this public holiday. I got through the hardship and to be able to take my mind off that inconvinience, we headed a Yakisoba restaurant which I used to visit in Takadanobaba. The taste clicked with me. I wish the restaurant in Takadanobaba could hang in there during Covid-19, but it couldn't. I know it's as good as it going to get. However, I was able to avoid the situation where I had to bang my head against the wall.

### 4. 文法・自然さフィードバック 📝

- 原文: I stayed up late last night **for playing** APEX Legends.
  - 修正案: I stayed up late last night **playing** APEX Legends.
  - 理由: stay up late + 動名詞で「〜しながら夜更かしする」。"for playing" は不要。

- 原文: I decided to **run to anywhere**.
  - 修正案: I decided to **just go for a run, anywhere would do**. / I decided to go running with no particular destination in mind.
  - 理由: "run to anywhere" は不自然。anywhere を目的地として "to" で結ばない。

- 原文: Ochano-mizu area, which is 4.2km **far from** my residence.
  - 修正案: Ochanomizu, which is 4.2km **away from** my place.
  - 理由: 距離は "away from" を使う。"far" は形容詞・副詞として使い、距離数値とは結ばない。

- 原文: I bumped into a small festival which **seemed a parade** of locals.
  - 修正案: ... which **seemed like a parade** of locals.
  - 理由: seem の後に名詞を取るときは "like" が必要。

- 原文: I had no clue that there would be **festival**.
  - 修正案: ... that there would be **a festival**.
  - 理由: 可算名詞 festival に冠詞 a が必須。

- 原文: First, I thought I **can** eat Okinawan soba.
  - 修正案: At first, I thought I **could** eat Okinawan soba.
  - 理由: 主節が過去形 (thought) なので時制一致で could。"First" より "At first"（最初は〜だった）の方が文脈に合う。

- 原文: ... we **headed a Yakisoba restaurant**.
  - 修正案: ... we **headed to a Yakisoba restaurant**.
  - 理由: head は自動詞用法では前置詞 to が必要。

- 原文: that **inconvinience**
  - 修正案: that **inconvenience**
  - 理由: スペル。

- 原文: I wish the restaurant in Takadanobaba **could hang in there** during Covid-19.
  - 修正案: I wish the restaurant in Takadanobaba **could have hung in there** through Covid-19.
  - 理由: 過去の事実に反する願望は仮定法過去完了 (could have + 過去分詞)。期間を示す前置詞は "through" が自然。

- 原文: I know it's **as good as it going to get**.
  - 修正案: I know it's **as good as it's going to get**.
  - 理由: it's (= it is) の 's が必要。
