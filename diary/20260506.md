In my sense I built by the recent running routine, 5 km is not so far and in the feasible scope. I left my apartment around 12 pm, and I arrived at Otemachi area around 1 pm. 

Just before running around the imperial palace, we had a lunch around there. First, we supposed to visit Bombei, which is a curry restaurant. However, since our money's a bit tight, so we went with a cheap soba resturant which is standing style. The taste was really delicious but I wanna try the signature menu which was sold out today. We had no luck.

Then we ran around the imperial palace for the first time in my life. I heard the length is 5 km. On the face of it, I felt that was too long to finish. The reality is, we finished in 35 mins at the end of the day. I saw some awkward people like thinking out loud. The boom of running gear will make me head there again for sure. I didn't have such an active hobby but I believe this time, this activity is in my nature to do. It's definitely my cup of tea. The best thing in running is the pros of getting me better mental mood. In addition to that, running gears are not so expensive as items used for other sports. Some influencers rub off on me. 

For whatever reason, I finally found my best bet to put my time for.

### 4. 文法・自然さフィードバック 📝

- `In my sense I built by the recent running routine` → **`Based on the sense I've built up through my recent running routine`**（`in my sense` は意味が通りにくい）
- `in the feasible scope` → **`within a feasible range`** / **`within reach`**
- `we had a lunch around there` → **`we had lunch around there`**（`lunch` は無冠詞）
- `First, we supposed to visit Bombei` → **`At first, we were supposed to visit Bombei`**（受動態 `were supposed to`。`First` だと「まず最初に」で次の行動を期待される）
- `However, since our money's a bit tight, so we went` → **`However, since money was a bit tight, we went`**（`since` と `so` は重複。1つに）
- `a cheap soba resturant which is standing style` → **`a cheap stand-up soba place`** / **`a cheap soba restaurant where you eat standing up`**（`resturant` → `restaurant` のスペル注意）
- `We had no luck.` → ○ 自然。`no luck` は会話/独り言で完結する定型句
- `I felt that was too long to finish` → **`I thought it would be too long to finish`**（実際に走る前の予想なので未来の推測 `would`）
- `in 35 mins at the end of the day` → **`in 35 mins in the end`**（`at the end of the day` は「結局のところ」で議論をまとめる時に使う表現。時間の経過には `in the end`）
- `I saw some awkward people like thinking out loud` → **`I saw some odd people who seemed to be thinking out loud`**（`like + V-ing` で「～のような」は不自然。関係代名詞か分詞構文に）
- `this activity is in my nature to do` → **`it's not out of my nature to enjoy this`** または **`this kind of activity actually suits me`**
- `the pros of getting me better mental mood` → **`the upside is that it puts me in a better mood`** / **`one pro is the boost to my mental state`**
- `running gears are not so expensive as items used for other sports` → **`running gear isn't as expensive as the equipment used for other sports`**（`gear` は不可算。`not so ~ as` より `not as ~ as` が自然）
- `my best bet to put my time for` → **`my best bet for spending my time on`** / **`the best thing to invest my time in`**（`put time for` は不自然）
